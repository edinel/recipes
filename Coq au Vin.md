# Coq au Vin

### Ingredients
* 1 &frac12; lbs Brown (cremini) Mushrooms
* &frac12; lb Maitake (hen-of-the-woods) mushrooms
* 3 large carrots
* 3 large onions
* A few sprigs of lemon thyme
* 1 bay leaf
* 2 quarts homemade chicken stock
* 4 cloves garlic
* 1 bottle red wine
* &frac12; cup cognac
* &frac12; lb bacon, cut into lardons
